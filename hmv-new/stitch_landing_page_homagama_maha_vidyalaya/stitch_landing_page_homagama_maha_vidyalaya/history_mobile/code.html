Our History

Tracing the path of excellence from a humble beginning to a premier seat of learning.

The
Foundation

Founded in the pivotal year of 1945, Homagama Maha Vidyalaya emerged as a beacon of hope during the post-war reconstruction era. What started with a handful of students and visionary teachers has now evolved into a cornerstone of Sri Lankan education.

1945

The Genesis

Originally established to provide quality education to the rural youth of Homagama, bridging the gap between urban prestige and local accessibility.

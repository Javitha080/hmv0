Our History

Established in 1889, Homagama Maha Vidyalaya has grown from a small village school to a premier educational institution with state-of-the-art facilities and a reputation for excellence.

Our alumni include renowned professionals, artists, and leaders who contribute to society in meaningful ways across various fields and disciplines.
